(Project created on May 2 2013)

This is a Conways Game of Life simulator (cgol is a little play on words :])

If you don't know what conways game of life IS... http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conway's_Game_of_Life

To create a map:

Open your favorite text editor
The first line is the height of the map, the second line is the width, and anything beyond that is the map itself
Here is one of the most simple creations...

~~~
5
5
-----
--X--
--X--
--X--
-----
~~~

Save that in the maps directory, and watch away.
